Sonicdot

This work aims to apply what audio scene analysis teaches us in order to synthesize a particular auditory scene: we want a listener to perceive a sound as intended but only at some designated point in the room. As soon as they move to a different position, the signal becomes unintelligible.

Work by Yu-Jeh Liu, Jonah Casebeer and Ivan Dokmanic.
